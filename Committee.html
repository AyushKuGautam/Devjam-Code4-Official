Committees

DARPAN

Darpan is the Dramatics Club of MNNIT.The dramatics club is home to all the theatre lovers and nukkad enthusiasts. A plethora of talented individuals join everyyear to take forward MNNIT's rich legacy in dramatics. Club majorly deals with Stage Plays and Nukkad. From writing play, to direction and to performance, members get to learn the skills required for each of these jobs.

QUINTESSENCE

Quintessence is also one of the most popular dramatics committee of MNNIT.Also known as 'Team Quinty',this club represents the total gusto of all the students here.This Cultural and Dramatics Society is an agglomeration of various societies of Music, dance, Vocals, Anchoring, Dramatics and Fashion. The club organized its annual event for the induction of freshers into the club in the month of November. The event was a celebration of the talent of the students in various genres of performing arts with successful candidates being absorbed into the clubs for organizing the further events.

MARATHI

Marathi culture revolves around dancing, and many folk dances are performed during festivals, marriage ceremonies, birth celebrations, and harvesting times. Lavani, Dhangarigaja, Lezim, Koli, and other traditional dances are popular in Maharashtra today. Lezim, Koli, Tamasha are performed by both male and female dancers whereas Lavani is performed by only females and Only males perform Dhangari Gaja.

BENGALI

With a culture as rich and multi-dimensional, the music composed and produced in Bengal is as rich and diverse as any other in the world. From classical to rock and folk to devotional along with a wide range of emotions- sadness, anxiety, love, devotional and spiritual that go hand in hand with it, music in Bengal encompasses and surpasses every genre.
